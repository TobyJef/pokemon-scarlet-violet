I Choose You

Ongoing Mightiest Mark Raid

Rock Terra Type Chesnaught

We are combating this bulky high physical attack and defence Pokemon with a Grass Terra Type Appletun, with Best IV's in all but Physical attack, Max EV's in Special Attack and Physical Defence while holding the Shell Bell item for health recovery. With a move set of Iron Defence, Apple Acid and Giga Drain.

Our full proof strategy to take down Chesnaught consists of a Turn One Iron Defence which we will use to counter Chesnaughts physical moves. Followed by consecutive Apple Acid attacks to fully lower Chesnaughts Special Defence, until we can Terrastalise our Grass type Appletun. Then either continue to use Apple Acid or use Giga Drain if your health starts to get low for the win.
