Ongoing Mightiest Mark Raid

Rock Terra Type Chesnaught the Unrivaled

We are combating this bulky high physical attack and defence Pokemon with a Level 100 special attacking Grass Terra Type Appletun. Bred or Bottle capped for Best IV's in all but it's physical attack and trained for Max EV's in Special Attack and Physical Defence while holding the Shell Bell item for health recovery. With only a three move set required of Iron Defence, Apple Acid and Giga Drain.

Our full proof strategy to take down Chesnaught consists of a Turn One Iron Defence which we will use to counter Chesnaughts physical moves. Followed by consecutive Apple Acid attacks to fully lower Chesnaughts Special Defence, until we can Terrastalise our Grass type Appletun. Thanks to our high physical defence stats and grass type resitance to Chesnaughts Rock and Ground type attacks as well as the held Shell Bell. Our tough little Appletun can withstand plenty of punishment from the assualting Chesnaught, so there is little risk of being KO'd and waiting to re-enter the raid. So continue to either spam our Unrivaled advisary with more Apple Acid attacks or use Giga Drain if your hp looks concerning for the win. A perfect raid for item farming Ability Patches and Capsules, Iron suppliments also Large and XL experience candies.

The Hidden Treasure of Area Zero

Part One - The Teal Mask

The Teal Mask comes as part one of The Hidden Treasure of Area Zero Expansion Pass. The Teal Mask sees your character go on a school trip to the land of Kitakami, a Japanese-inspired land which sits at the base of a great mountain and is full of rice paddies and apple orchards. Your trip seems to coincide with a festival regularly held in the village in Kitakami. The village is bustling with various street vendors and stalls. You’ll meet new friends and Pokémon as you unravel the mysteries behind the folktales that have been passed down in this land.

Scheduled for release Fall 2023

Part 2 - The Indigo Disk

The Indigo Disk will have you attend the Blueberry Academy, the Naranja/Uva Academy's sister school. The Blueberry Academy is a relatively new school, and while there, you'll take classes and meet a whole host of new characters. There are a few standout features of this new school, its curriculum puts a particular emphasis on Pokémon battling. Most surprising of all is the fact that most of the school’s building is located under the ocean.

Scheduled for release Winter 2023

Those that purchase the upcoming content before October 31st 2023 are rewarded with a code for a Dark Terra type Hisuian Zoroark.
