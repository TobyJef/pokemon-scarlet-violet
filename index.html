Pokeviews on Pokemon Scarlet and Violet

Seven months have now passed since the release of Pokemon Scarlet and Pokemon Violet. The ninth entry into the Pokemon franchise and the second main series game to be released on the Nintendo Switch. Following mixed reviews from fans and critics alike of Pokemon Sword and Pokemon Shield and the mostly positive reception of Pokemon Legends Arceus. Have Nintendo and Gamefreak listened to the feedback? Produce what could be one of the best games in the franchise, or rush a game onto the ageing Switch hardware knowing that come release day, they can sit back and watch the revenue come streaming in as their legions of fans will empty the shelves of their local gaming stores or feverishly wait for the complete of the download from the Nintendo eShop.

Well the team here at Pokeviews have you covered, we have extensively played through the main story and post game content, completed the Paldea Pokedex, tested our might against the Mightiest Mark Terra Raids and tried out all the new features the game has to offer. So join us over on the review page for our full honest take on Pokemon Scarlet and Violet, where we break down our review to provide a detailed, fair and balanced review for old and new Pokefans alike.
