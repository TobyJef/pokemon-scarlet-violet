Pokeviews on Pokemon Scarlet and Violet

Seven months have now passed since the release of Pokemon Scarlet and Pokemon Violet. The ninth entry into the Pokemon franchise and the second main series game to be released on the Nintendo Switch. After mixed reviews from fans and critics alike of Pokemon Sword and Pokemon Shield, followed by the mostly positive reception of Pokemon Legends Arceus. Have Nintendo and Gamefreak listened to the feedback? To produce what could be one of the best games in the Pokemon franchise, or deploy a game onto the ageing Switch hardware knowing that come release day, they can sit back and watch the revenue come streaming in as their legions of fans will empty the shelves of their local gaming stores or feverishly wait for the complete of the download from the Nintendo eShop.

Well the team here at Pokeviews have you covered, we have extensively played through the main story and post game content, completed the 400 strong Paldean Pokedex, tested our might against the Mightiest Mark Terra Raids and tested the new features these games have to offer.
